ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Niko Yaitanes—a creative professional with a passion for media, visual storytelling, brand strategy, and digital innovation. Media is at the forefront of who I am. I inject inspiration from media consumption into everything I create, leveraging trends to amplify content, ensuring quality outcomes, and quantifiable results.        

I have a Bachelor of Science in Communication with a Concentration in Cinema and Media Studies from Boston University. I have a developer's certifcation in Responsive Web Design (300 Hour Certification, HTML and CSS).     

When I'm not working, I am a coffee fiend, enjoy traveling, and the ocean. I also have a New Music Friday playlist that I update weekly.
